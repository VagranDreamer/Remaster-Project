üí•NEW VISUAL STYLE


The vanilla DAO was extremely gloomy, of the same type, and overly clogged. Our task was to fix it. We were inspired by the latest Bioware studio games: DAI and DAV. And here is our result: a variety of colors, variability of vegetation, clear textures without grain, steel glitters, and ladies enchant with their beauty. At the same time, the circle of magicians, caves and deep trails retained their former dark and cold appearance.

üü•BLOCK STRUCTURE


All mods are divided into categories, which will be very easy to navigate and make changes to your liking. For example, to organize a change of texture pack to vanilla, remove annoying faces, return old music, bodies, etc. You can install what you need from above by forming your own assembly. We advise only experienced users to do this, if you are a beginner, it is better to leave everything as it is.
