The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt Remaster Project

TW3RP (Old-Gen) contain 160+ mods, fixes and small embedded programs for easy use. Immerse yourself in the life of the Northern Kingdoms!

ABOUT MOD PACK

Monsters are threatening the world of Neverland again, take the battle and decide for yourself who is worthy of your sword or your mercy! Take a look at Geralt's journey and appreciate the new graphic features.


    The assembly includes:

  • -High resolution textures and new detailed models
  • -A new rendering system that adds reflections, sun glare and grass shadows
  • -New clothing options for the characters
  • -Custom editing of Geralt's head in TW2 style (facial animations are normal)
  • -New animations of eating, wounding, loot collection and other interaction with the world
  • -E3-style walking and running animations
  • -Customizable fighting style and rolling
  • -Various weather effects, improved fog, smoke, rain and snow
  • -Modified gwent system (it became more difficult in the initial stages)
  • -A number of visual changes (runes, signs, flair, realistic powerful punches without lines, removal of annoying splashes of dirt on the screen)
  • -More blood splashes from punches
  • -New shaders instead of a Reshade
  • -New UI and customizable camera
  • -New lighting for all regions
  • -Fixes and tweaks

The original Witcher 2015 was certainly one of the most beautiful games of its time. But if we judge in isolation from the sympathy for this wonderful game, we can conclude that it was performed in very warm colors, so warm that sometimes it seems that everything around has a yellow tint. We fixed this problem by adding one of the best lighting mods. Now the sand and roads have a more natural color, the sunlight has become many times more realistic.

üü•BLOCK STRUCTURE


All mods are divided into categories, which will be very easy to navigate and make changes to your liking. For example, to organize a change of texture pack to vanilla, remove annoying faces, return old UI, hairs, etc. You can install what you need from above by forming your own assembly. We advise only experienced users to do this, if you are a beginner, it is better to leave everything as it is.
